Healthy Japanese Fried Rice

By Foodieland | 20 minutes | Serves 2

Nutrition Facts
  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbs: 34g
  • Fat: 6g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup diced vegetables
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Other Recipes
Spicy Chicken Wings Vegetarian Tacos Classic Caesar Salad

Directions

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side and scramble the eggs in the same pan.
  4. Mix the cooked rice and soy sauce, then combine everything together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
